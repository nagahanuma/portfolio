3D models folder.

To replace the fallback procedural cyber-drone, please drop your custom 3D model (in `.glb` or `.gltf` format) here and name it:
`cyber_drone.glb`

The javascript logic in `chat-agent.js` will automatically detect, load, and style it with your portfolio's theme colors (Matte Slate Grey body, glowing neon cyan accents/lights, and periwinkle ambient highlights).
